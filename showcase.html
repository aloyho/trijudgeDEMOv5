Showcase Judge — How to Use
Quick Guide
PARTICIPANT SEQUENCE
Paste your group lineup (numbered, comma-separated, or plain lines). After loading, navigate with Prev / Next — the group name auto-fills as you move through. Click any chip to jump directly. After locking a score it auto-advances to the next entry.
SHOWCASE TYPE
Choose Open Showcase or Kpop Cover. Each type loads different default criteria. You can edit these any time.
CRITERIA & WEIGHTAGE
Edit Criteria — toggle items on/off or add custom ones. Set Weightage — assign a % to each criterion (must total 100%). Use Distribute Evenly for a quick equal split. The live summary always shows your current weights.
SCORING
Use Slider mode to drag, or Direct Key-in to type. Each criterion's max score equals its weight %. The weighted total out of 100 calculates automatically. Lock Score to save. Tap any saved entry to expand and edit individual criterion scores or the group name.
EXPORT
Tap + → Share / Export to copy results as text or download as CSV.
